Dear Mircea,

Thank you for writing and wanting to know more of VentureConnect.

The methodolody is very simple:

- you need to fill in the application form attached to the present email and to send it to projects@ventureconnect.ro by 3rd of June

- by 15th of June, the pre-selected projects will be announced (meaning that the start-ups which the VentureConnect Advisory Board finds promising, will have the possibility of presenting their business plan on 20th of June in front of a wide audience of entreprenours and investors from US and Europe)

- before the presentation itself, the entreprenours present in the pitch will benefit from an in-house training from the VentureConnect board (how to maximize their presentations and how to convince the audience they are the ones to receive the investment funds)

I look forward to your project. Please, let me know if there are any questions which I can help with.

Thank you kindly,
Alexandra
