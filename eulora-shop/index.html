    Note that products in which I am not well stocked (to a value of 10 mn ECu or more) will be sold through open outcry auction in #eulora rather than directly. The opening price will be as quoted above.

    Note that prices are quoted as "run of the mill quality", meaning whatever quality I produce. Most crafted items can be produced to a quality of your specification for no extra cost. In some circumstances (if I don't have precursors or blueprints of the right qualities) this may result in a surcharge. Feel free to approach me to negotiate the matter.

    Note that crafted items may be subject to blueprint rarity surcharge, depending on which blueprints are difficult to source at whatever point in time. You can always avoid the surcharge as well as ensure a ~iv45% discount off the price of the final item through providing the blueprints yourself.
